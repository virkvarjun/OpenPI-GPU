## Instrument corrections (this file supersedes the earlier artifact numbers)

Two XLA-metric bugs were found and fixed (see DISPATCH_PROFILE.md):
1. **device-busy** now = the median **blocked** step time (211.9 ms), not the sum of Chrome-trace op durations
   (which under-counted ~12× and manufactured a phantom 91% "data-wait"). data-wait is now correctly ~0.
2. **MFU** now = **6·N·D / device_time** (the scaling-paper formula: 3.24B params × 3464 tok/step) = 32.1% on
   H100, not `cost_analysis()['flops']` (which under-reported ~100× → a nonsense 0.2%).
The op-category *composition* still comes from the trace (relative proportions), scaled to the real device time.

## Conclusion (unchanged, now on solid numbers)

- **gemma_2b trains at ~32% MFU on one H100** (batch 4, full AdamW) — decent for a naive-attention VLA.
- **matmul-FFN dominates (~58%); attention softmax ≈ 0%** → no fused-attention headroom; XLA saturates the
  GEMMs. There is **no single-GPU step-level optimization headroom**; the lever is throughput via more devices
  (Sharder V1's multi-host FSDP — validated on 4×H100, see MULTIHOST_VALIDATION.md).
